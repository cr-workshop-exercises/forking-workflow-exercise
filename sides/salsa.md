# Salsa recipe


## Ingredients

- 250g-300g tomatoes (cherry tomatoes are best because they have less water) 
- 1 hot pepper (recomended habanero, but jalapeno or others also work)
- 1 lime
- 1 clove of garlic
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- 1/2 red onion
- 3 Tbsp cilantro


## Instructions

- in a pan dry fry all of the ingredients except lime and cilantro
 (best to do each ingredient separately)
- put all the ingredients in a container, add the lime and cilantro and blend
 with an immersion blender to desired consistency.
- enjoy!
