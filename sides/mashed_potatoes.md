# Mashed potatoes
## Ingredients:

- 1 kg potatoes (Yukon Gold or similar)
- 4 tbsp butter
- ½ cup milk (warm)
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions:

Peel and cut potatoes into chunks.
Boil in salted water until very soft (about 15–20 min).
Drain the potatoes and return them to the hot pot to steam off excess moisture.
Mash with butter first (it gives a richer taste).
Add warm milk gradually until you reach your preferred texture.
Season with salt and pepper, and mix gently until smooth.
