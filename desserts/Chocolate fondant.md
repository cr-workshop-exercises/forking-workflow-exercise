# Chocolate fondant recipe

## Ingredients
- Dark chocolate: 100g
- Wheat flour: 300g
- Eggs: 1 medium 
- Butter: 50g

## Instructions
- Melt half of chocolate on low fire
- Mix butter and melted chocolate
- Slowly add and mix egg into butter-chocolate mix
- Add flour and mix everything thoroughly
- Add dough into muffin molds and insert piece of chocolate in the middle of your cake
- Bake at temperature 200 degrees C for 10 minutes
