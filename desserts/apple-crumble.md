# Apple crumble recipe

## Ingredients

Crumble dough:
- 100 g oats
- 100 g unsalted butter
- 1 tbsp of molten unsalten butter
- 125 g flour
- 100 g white sugar
- a dash of salt
- a knifepoint of cinnamon


Filling:
- 4 Granny Smith apples (large)
- 35 g of white sugar
- 1 tbsp of lime juice
- 1 tsp of cinnamon
- 1 tbsp of molten butter

## Instructions

- Preheat the oven to 175 ºC
- Peel and dice the apples and put them in a bowl
- Add lime juice, cinnamon, sugar and molten butter. Stir thoroughly
- Weigh the ingredients for the crumble dough and put them in a separate bowl
- Mix the ingredients with your fingers until a crumbly dough takes shape. Don't knead, that'll turn it into one cohesive dough, but let it run through your fingers
- Put the apple mixture into an oven pan
- Add the crumble dough on top, and place the pan in the oven for 40 minutes. The top should become slightly golden brown
