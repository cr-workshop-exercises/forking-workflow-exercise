# Chocolate mousse recipe

## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 200g dark chocolate
- 1 pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Separate the yolks from the egg whites.
2. Beat the eggs whites until stiff (with a pinch of salt; the eggs should not be too cold if they were stored in the fridge).
3. Melt the chocolate.
4. Wait for the melted chocolate to cool down, and mix the yolks to the cooled melted chocolate.
5. Add the foamy egg whites to the chocolate, slowly.
6. (Optional: put the preparation into small dishes for individual portions)
7. Let it sit in the fridge for at least 6 hours
