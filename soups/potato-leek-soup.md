# This delicious potato-leek soup is creamy, filling, and perfect for cold days.

# Ingredients
- 500 g potatoes
- 2-3 leeks
- 1-2 onion
- 2 cloves garlic (optional)
- 1 liter chicken stock or vegetable broth
- 2-3 dl cream
- 100 g bacon (optional)
- Butter for frying
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh chives for garnish

# Instructions
## Preparation: 
- Peel the potatoes and cut them into smaller cubes. 
- Clean the leeks and slice them thinly. 
- Chop the onion and garlic coarsely.

## Sautéing: 
- Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat. 
- Add the potatoes, leeks, onion, and garlic, and let them sauté for about 5 minutes, until they are slightly soft but not brown.

## Boiling: 
- Add chicken stock or broth and bring the soup to a boil.
- Let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes and leeks are tender.

## Blending: 
- Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until completely smooth.
- If you want a more rustic soup, you can leave some pieces whole.

## Add cream: 
- Add the cream to the soup and bring it back to a boil.
- Season with salt and pepper.

## Bacon (optional): 
- Fry bacon pieces in a pan until crispy.
- Let them drain on paper towels.

## Serving: 
- Serve the soup steaming hot, topped with crispy bacon and finely chopped chives.
- You can also serve a good piece of bread with it.
