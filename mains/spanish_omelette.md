# Spanish omelette

## Ingredients

- 4–5 medium potatoes
- 1 small onion
- 5–6 eggs
- 200–250 ml olive oil
- Salt

## Instructions

- Peel and thinly slice the potatoes (and onion if using)
- Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat
- Cook potatoes and onion gently until soft (15–20 min, not browned)
- Remove and drain excess oil
- Beat eggs with salt in a bowl
- Add potatoes/onion to eggs and mix gently
- Let mixture rest for 5 minutes
- Heat a little oil in a pan and pour in mixture
- Cook until bottom is set
- Flip using a plate and cook other side (Flip as many times you think)
- Rest briefly, then slice and serve. Enjoy!
