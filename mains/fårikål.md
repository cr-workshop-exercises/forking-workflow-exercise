# Fårikål

## Ingredients
- 1 cabbage
- 1 kg mutton in large dices (with bones)
- Pepper seeeds
- Potatoes

## Instructions
- Fry mutton
- Cut cabbage into large pieces
- Cook mutton and cabbage together with pepper until bones are easy to take out. This takes maybe one hour, maybe three.
- Serve with cooked potatoes
