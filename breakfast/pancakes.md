# Pancakes

## Ingredients
- 2 eggs
- 2 dl flour
- 2 dl milk
- 1 tbsp vanila sugar
- 1 tsp baking powder
- A pinch of salt
- Butter (or oil or margarine) for frying
- Bacon

## Instructions
1. Mix ingredients
2. Fry in pan
3. Serve with jam, nutella, maple syrup and/or berries!
4. Add some cinnamon
